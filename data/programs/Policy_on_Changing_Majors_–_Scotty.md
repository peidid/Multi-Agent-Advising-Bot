# Policy on Changing Majors – Scotty

Page owner: Jarrin F Nevel  
Last Updated on August 31, 2025 by Ryan D Riley

CMU-Q students who wish to apply for internal transfer into another department/major must follow the steps below and complete the application prior to the application deadline. Applications may be submitted in both the fall and spring semesters for all students except those in their first year. 

First Year Students should submit their [application form](https://scotty.qatar.cmu.edu/wp-content/uploads/2024/07/CMUQ-Internal-Transfer-Application-9Jan25.pdf) after final grades have been submitted for their first year. (In May of the first year)

Sophomore students and above should submit their [application form](https://scotty.qatar.cmu.edu/wp-content/uploads/2024/07/CMUQ-Internal-Transfer-Application-9Jan25.pdf) no later than 2 weeks prior to registration week in order to be considered for the following semester.
