### Eligibility:

This award is open to currently enrolled CMUQ undergraduate students who were hired to work as Course Assistants with the Academic Resource Center during the current academic year.

### How to Nominate:

Nominations may come from any undergraduate student enrolled in CMUQ. Course Assistants cannot nominate themselves for the award. Students who would like to see their Course Assistant nominated for the award are encouraged to submit a nomination by completing the Nomination form [here](https://forms.qatar.cmu.edu/award-for-outstanding-course-assistant/).

Once all nominations are collected by the Director of the Academic Resource Center, the ARC Staff and Associate Dean for Education will review the nominations and recommendation letters received. The selection committee will only consider nominations that are complete and received before the deadline.

### Selection Criteria:

This award is given to individuals who have demonstrated the knowledge, skills, and commitment to students learning outcomes both in and out of the classroom. The Selection Committee considers the following criteria:

-   Demonstrates knowledge of the field and displays a commitment to student success  -   shows interest in the subject and is engaging and enthusiastic
      -   explains the course content facts and concepts clearly and logically
      -   goes above and beyond to assist students in terms of scheduling flexibility, or structures instruction to meet the needs of the student
-   Facilitates a supportive learning environment  -   provides support and guidance to students, thus enabling them to discover answers on their own
      -   models excellent problem-solving strategies
      -   encourages student participation, engages and motivates students
      -   provides timely, constructive feedback
      -   respects students’ diverse backgrounds and values
-   Shows evidence of working in a professional manner with students, faculty supervisors, and ARC Staff  -   works in a collegial manner with students, faculty, ARC Staff and other Course Assistants
      -   demonstrates high standards, ethics, and academic integrity
      -   exhibits excellent organization skills
      -   works to improve the learning environment

### Tips to Complete a Well-Written Nomination:

Supporting evidence strengthens the nomination. The nominating member is encouraged to gather as much supporting evidence as possible. For example,

Students can:

-   Tell about one or two tutoring sessions they had observed with the Course Assistant that was particularly noteworthy. Although merely describing the sessions is not the point. Articulate a particular characteristic or behavior they embodied that impressed you will strengthen your nomination.

Faculty can:

-   Focus on specific examples that best describe their personal attitude and professional skills
-   Elaborate on any observations/activities that demonstrate the Course Assistant’s leadership qualities
