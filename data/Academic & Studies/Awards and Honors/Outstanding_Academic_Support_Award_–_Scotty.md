# Outstanding Academic Support Award – Scotty

Page owner: Shamila Abdul Khader  
Last Updated on March 9, 2025 by Dudley Reynolds

The _Outstanding Academic Support Award_ honors staff members involved in courses for their commitment to students’ academic excellence. These are staff members who:

-   Support students’ academic success and excellence;
-   Foster student learning and academic development;
-   Contribute to teaching and learning by way of curriculum design, content, and delivery.

### Eligibility of Nominees

The following staff members are eligible for being nominated for this award:

-   Teaching Assistants for the majors and for Arts and Sciences;
-   Post-docs or research assistants **who are involved in courses**.

Nominees must have supported courses at CMU-Q for at least two full semesters by the time of nomination (i.e., they must have been involved with courses for at least the semesters of Fall 2024 and Spring 2025.

Check out past winners [here.](https://scotty.qatar.cmu.edu/dedication-to-students-academic-excellence-award/dedication-to-students-academic-excellence-award-previous-winner/)

### Nomination Deadline

Nominations must be received no later than March 20, 2025, midnight.

### Nomination Process

Faculty, supervisors of Teaching Assistants, and students may submit nominations.

Students, faculty, and supervisors may nominate one Teaching Assistant only. Multiple nominations by the same nominator will be disregarded.

Nominations can be submitted online  through this **[nomination page](https://forms.gle/FyWu5WajXoHB31tR6).**  Once applications close, a committee will convene and select the winner for the Outstanding Academic Support Award.
