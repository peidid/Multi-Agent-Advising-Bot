### **Dean’s List  
**

Students across all majors offered at CMU in Qatar earn the Dean’s List recognition in a given semester by achieving one of two minimum standards.

1-             They must either earn a semester QPA of 3.75 or higher (while taking at least 36 factorable units and receiving no incomplete grades)

2-            Or earn a semester QPA of 3.50 or higher (while taking at least 45 factorable units and receiving no incomplete grades).
