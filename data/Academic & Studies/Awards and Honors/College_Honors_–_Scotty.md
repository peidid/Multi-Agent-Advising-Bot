### **College Honors**

College honors are conferred to a student who completes a senior honors thesis. Criteria for admission into a college honors program varies according to the student’s major.  College honors are mentioned on the transcripts.

-   **Dietrich  College  –  Cord color is purple**  -   Information Systems Students are awarded Dietrich Humanities & Social Sciences senior honors if they maintain a QPA of 3.25 or higher, a QPA of 3.5 within their major, complete a thesis, and present it at Meeting of the Minds.
    
-   **Mellon College of Science  –  Cord color is yellow**  -   Biological Sciences students are awarded MCS Research Honors if they maintain a QPA of 3.2 or higher, demonstrate quality undergraduate research, and either publish or present their research at a scientific meeting.
    
-   **School of Computer Science  –  Cord color is tiffany gold**  -   Computer Science students complete a senior research thesis under the guidance of a faculty member. The students present their research results at the annual Meeting of the Minds Undergraduate Research Symposium.
    
-   **Tepper School of Business – Cord color is drab brown**  -   Business Administration Students are awarded the Tepper College Honors if they maintain a QPA of 3.75 or higher, and complete a senior research thesis under the guidance of a faculty member.  Students may present their research results at the annual Meeting of the Minds Undergraduate.
