### **University Honors**

Students maintaining a cumulative QPA of at least 3.5 after seven semesters of full-time enrollment, or raising their QPA to 3.5 upon completing their graduation requirements in their final semester, receive University Honors. University honors are mentioned on the diploma. Students will be presented with a medallion to wear with their regalia.
