### **What is _Majāl_?**

_[Majāl](https://majal.lps.library.cmu.edu/)_ is a peer-reviewed undergraduate journal devoted to cultivating and disseminating the wide range of scholarly endeavors pursued by undergraduate students from Carnegie Mellon University’s Qatar campus. _Majāl_ is published annually on a rolling basis. 

_Majāl_ editors mentor students through the publishing process, prioritizing student growth as they follow a rigorous double-blind review and publication process to develop their best work.

### **Who can publish in _Majāl_?**

Students from all CMU-Q majors can submit work to Majal. CMU-Q students can have co-authors who are faculty or students from the Pittsburgh campus. CMU-Q alumni may also publish for up to two years after graduation. 

### **What can I submit to _Majāl_?**

_Majāl_ welcomes three types of submissions from student authors:

1.  independent student research, such as theses, capstones, or other projects
2.  course-based projects
3.  Student and faculty co-authored collaborations

###   
**What does the publication process look like?**

##### 1\. Submit your work to _Majāl_’s online platform. 

Submission requires that you create a log-in to the Janeway publication platform and fill out a linked google form. 

##### 2\. Meet with _Majāl_ editors to understand the review process.

Majal editors will meet with you for 15-20 minutes to explain the steps and expectations of publication. 

##### 3\. Wait for your submissions’ reviews and an editorial decision letter. 

_Majāl_ editors will send a letter containing at least 2 expert reviews and a review summary to guide your revisions. 

##### 4\. Revise and resubmit your manuscript.

Sometimes revision includes only one pass, but often it takes several. Majal editors will work closely with you for support. 

##### 5\. Receive an acceptance letter for publication & approve your formatted proofs. 

Editors will send a formal acceptance letter when your manuscript has been approved. They will then send you a formatted version of your manuscript for approval before publication. 

##### 6\. Publication

Your manuscript will be assigned a DOI and will be permanently searchable on Google Scholar and other platforms. 

_Majāl_ is published with Creative Commons Licensing through [CMU Library Publishing Services](https://lps.library.cmu.edu/). Aside from CMU’s right to host your article on their publishing website, all rights to the work are held by the author(s). Authors can publish their work elsewhere after Majāl provided future venues have no objection to previously published work. 

### **Can I work for Majal?**

Yes! _Majāl_ employs at least one Student Editor each year. Watch for advertisements through the ARC’s non-academic worker postings.
