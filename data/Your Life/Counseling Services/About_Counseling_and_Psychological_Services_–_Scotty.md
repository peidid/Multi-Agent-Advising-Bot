### About Counseling and Psychological Services-Qatar (CaPS-Q)

CaPS-Q offers ethical, professional and confidential counseling and psychological services to CMU students. These services are geared towards assisting students to develop solutions for their emotional, academic and interpersonal challenges which may include but are not limited to, feelings of sadness and low mood, anxiety and stress, adjustment/transition issues, time management issues, procrastination, learning difficulties, loss/grief, low energy, low self-esteem, relationship and family issues, body image and eating disorders. Referrals for specialized services (ie. psychotropic medication consultation, nutrition, etc.) are also made when needed.
