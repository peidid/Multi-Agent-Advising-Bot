# Emergency Student Loans and Grants – Scotty

Page owner: Dalia Atef Rehal  
Last Updated on December 16, 2024 by Angela Ford

Interest free Emergency Student Loans and Grants are coordinated by Student Affairs. The Emergency Student Loan is a repayable emergency-based loan or a non-repayable grant available to enrolled students for academic supplies, medication, food or other unforeseeable circumstances. Tuition costs are not eligible for Emergency Student Loans or Grants.

Please follow these instructions to apply for an emergency student loan or grant:

Use the link below to open the student loan and grant application.

[Emergency Student Funding](https://xforms.andrew.cmu.edu/StudentGrantsLoanRequestQatar)

-   Enter your Andrew ID in the appropriate field and hit tab. The form will fill in your Name, Email, Class, and Preferred First Name (if applicable). If you do not have a Preferred First Name, enter your First Name in this field.
-   Enter your phone number in the Best Contact Phone Number field.
-   Enter the amount of funding you are requesting. Please provide your best estimate as you will further discuss your financial needs when you meet with a member of the Emergency Student Funding team.
-   Provide a brief description of the purpose of the needed funding meaning how the funds are intended to be used (i.e. Meals or other living expense, medical needs etc.). You will further discuss your needs when you meet with a loan authorizer.
-   Read the terms of the loan and click Submit Form. Your official name of CMU record will appear in the Student’s Signature box along with today’s date. A dialog box will appear with the message “Thank you for your submission. It has been successfully submitted.” You do not need to record the tracking number. If needed, please reference the loan number located at the top right of the form (QESL00000\_\_\_\_).

You will receive an email confirming that your Emergency Student Funding request was successfully submitted. A member of the review team will contact you within three business days. If your situation is urgent, or you have not received a response within this period, please email Dean Dalia at [drehal@andrew.cmu.edu](mailto:drehal@andrew.cmu.edu) reference your application number. If you need additional support regarding your situation, please do not hesitate to contact Student Affairs immediately. We will help you to determine the best path forward.
