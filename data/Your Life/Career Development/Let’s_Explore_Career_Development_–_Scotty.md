We are dedicated to helping you explore your career options and develop the essential skills required for professional success.

### _**About this Asynchronous Course**_

This course contains core learning to help build your employability and develop your career readiness. The modules are designed to help you explore your career options, develop the skills you need to succeed in the workplace, and make connections with employers and opportunities. You have the flexibility to do this at any time and at your own pace.

[https://canvas.cmu.edu/enroll/9CNTWT](https://canvas.cmu.edu/enroll/9CNTWT)

[**Focus 2 – Career Exploration Assessment Tool**](https://scotty.qatar.cmu.edu/homepage-3/your-life-at-cmu-q/meet-the-team/student-affairs/career-development-office/focus-2-career-exploration-tool/)

**Focus 2 Career** is a  career planning and college major exploration tool. You can combine the results to help you map out your future options, looking at job profiles, salaries (US), and requirements needed in these career paths.
