**Internal Transfer Application**

CMU-Q students who wish to apply for internal transfer into another department/major must follow the steps below and complete the application prior to the application deadline. Applications may be submitted in both the fall and spring semesters for all students except those in their first year. [#internal-transfer](#internal-transfer)

First Year Students should submit their [application form](https://scotty.qatar.cmu.edu/wp-content/uploads/2024/07/CMUQ-Internal-Transfer-Application-9Jan25.pdf) after final grades have been submitted for their first year.

Sophomore students and above should submit their [application](https://scotty.qatar.cmu.edu/wp-content/uploads/2024/07/CMUQ-Internal-Transfer-Application-9Jan25.pdf) no later than 2 weeks prior to registration week in order to be considered for the following semester.
